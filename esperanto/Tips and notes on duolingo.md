#Verbs: -ig/-i?
Tips and notes
##-ig 和 -i?
-ig和-i?词缀表示状态的变化，-ig意味着引起变化，-i?意味着经历变化，或者从一种状态变成另一种：
|Esperanto |English |
|--|--|
|ru?a |red |
|ru?igi |to make something red |
|ru?i?i |to become red, to blush |
|bela |beautiful |
|beligi |to make something beautiful |
|beli?i |to become beautiful 

以上的例子展示了两种词缀和形容词的组合方式，它们也可以用在动词上：
|Esperanto |English |
|--|--|
|man?i |to eat |
|man?igi |to make someone eat, to feed |
|morti |to die |
|mortigi |to make someone die, to kill |
|levi |to lift, to raise |
|levi?i |to rise |
|veki |to wake someone up |
|veki?i |to wake up |

通过以上例子可以看出，-ig的动词都有宾语，而-i?没有。即-ig动词是及物动词，-i?动词是不及物动词。
英语中许多动词同时既是及物又是不及物动词，但是在世界语中不是这样，一个动词通常要么及物要么不及物。英语里可以说The girl closed the window和The window closed，用同一个close就可以。但世界语中需要区分开，malfermi, fermi, komenci, fini, ?angi, movi都是及物动词，而其不及物动词则需要-i?词缀：
|English |Transitive |Intransitive|
|--|--|--|
|to close |fermi |fermi?i|
|to open |malfermi |malfermi?i|
|to start,begin |komenci |komenci?i|
|to finish,end |fini |fini?i|
|to change |?an?i |?an?i?i|
|to move |movi |movi?i|

以下是一些用上面词的例子：
|Esperanto |English|
|--|--|
|?i ru?i?is. |She blushed.|
|?i beligis mian vivon. |She made my life beautiful.|
|?u vi man?igis la infanojn? |Did you feed the children?|
|La suno levi?as. |The sun is rising.|
|Mia hundo provas veki min, sed mi ne volas veki?i |My dog is trying to wake me up, but I don't want to wake up.|
|La knabo fermis la fenestron. |The boy closed the window.|
|La fenestro fermi?is. |The window closed (by itself).|
|La studentoj komencis la lecionon. |The students started (doing) the lesson.|
|La leciono komenci?as je la na?a. |The lesson starts at 9 (o'clock).|

也可以在动词后面加-i?变成不及物动词，比如sidi（to sit, be sitting）和ku?i (to lie, be lying down)，-i?表示一种变化，切换到全新的状态：
- Li sidi?is sur la sofo.    He sat down on the sofa. (He "became sitting" on the sofa.)
- La hundo ku?i?is sur la planko.    The dog lay down on the floor. (The dog "became lying" on the floor.)

##Dolori al iu....
世界语中，可以说dolori al来表示一个人身体某个部位痛：
Doloras al mi la kapo. 或 "La kapo doloras al mi. 或 Doloras min la kapo. 或 La kapo doloras min.
My head hurts. (头是主语)
La brako doloras al ?i. 或 La brako doloras ?in.
Her arm hurts. (胳膊是主语).
也可以说Mia kapo doloras或?ia brako doloras，就像英语一样。

##物主代词也可以用La来代替
当谈论某人的身体、衣服、私人财产等时，la可以代替物主代词。通常在上下文很清楚时使用，比如：
- Mi finfine trovis la okulvitrojn. = I finally found my glasses.
- La kapo doloras al mi. = My head hurts.
- Lavu la manojn! = Wash your hands!
##Kontra?
之前学过kontra?可以表示against或opposite。在医学方面，也可以表示for the purpose of treating or curing：
Mi prenis medikamenton kontra? febro.
I took a medication for fever.

##Ekzerci sin : 反身形式
英语里，主语宾语相同时，会用一种特殊的反身代词，XXXself。
世界语没有第一、第二人称的反身代词。
|Non-reflexive |Reflexive|
|--|--|
|?i vidas min - She sees me |Mi vidas min - I see myself|
|?i vidas nin - She sees us |Ni vidas nin - We see ourselves|
|?i vidas vin - She sees you |Vi vidas vin - You see yourself|
世界语仅有一种反身代词，si，用作第三人称。
?i vidas sin ("She sees herself"; the person who is being seen is the same as the person who is seeing.) -?i vidas ?in ("She sees her"; the person being seen is not the same as the person who is seeing.)
si是中性词，并且单复数通用。
|Non-reflexive |Reflexive|
|--|--|
|?i vidas ?in - She sees her (another female person) |?i vidas sin - She sees herself|
|Li vidas lin - He sees him (another male person) |Li vidas sin - He sees himself|
|Ili vidas ilin - They see them (another group of people) |Ili vidas sin - They see themselves|

#Travel
##Ju (mal)pli...des (mal)pli
相当于the more... the more或the less...the less，Ju通常在前，des在后：
- Ju pli da mono, des pli da zorgo. (The more money, the more worry.)
- Ju pli frue, des pli bone. (The earlier, the better.)
- Ju malpli mi laboras, des malpli mi volas labori. (The less I work, the less I want to work.)
- Ju pli li atentas, des malpli li komprenas. (The more he pays attention, the less he understands.)
- Ju malpli da vortoj, des pli bone. (The fewer words, the better.)

##Veturi and Voja?i
Voja?i意思是travel，一般的概括用词。Veturi比较特殊，特指用交通工具的旅行，而不包括那些徒步的旅行：
- Kiam vi voja?os al ?inio? = When will you travel to China?
- Ni preferas veturi al Berlino per a?to. = We prefer to travel to Berlin by car.
注意：Veturilo指交通工具，任何运人运货的机器都可以算作交通工具，比如aviadilo (aircraft), motorciklo (motorcycle), a?to (car)。

##trans和transiri后的-n
Trans意思是across, on the far side。当表示方向时，不要忘记在名词加上-n。比较下面的例子：
- Mia domo trovi?as trans la strato.  My house is located across the street.
- La infano kuras trans la straton.   The child is running across the street (to the other side of the street).

Transiri意思是to go across：
- Kial la koko transiris la vojon?
- Why did the chicken cross the road?

##Barato
世界语中传统对印度的叫法是Hindujo/Hindio。然而现在最喜欢用的却是Barato，是婆罗多（梵语中对印度的称呼）的世界语叫法。在世界语文章中，三种叫法都可能有用到。
##Vendejo 和 butiko
卖店、商店叫做butiko，而Vendejo则是其的一般概括用词，它还可以包括批发大卖场之类的意思。Butiko通常指的则是零售店，当意指零售店时，也可以用vendejo。
##Pasporta Servo
Pasporta Servo是由世界青年世界语协会（EJO）在1966年建立的一个好客的网站，有来自90多个国家上千名主人提供免费寄宿。一些世界语者利用此网站进行便宜的环球旅行或与世界的朋友们在家里见面。2009年，服务上线，可以在www.pasportaservo.org访问。

#Education
##分词
分词用来构成更复杂的动词形态，表示各种时态。它由动词变化而来，有主动和被动两种，每种又分现在、将来、过去时。
|Tense |Active participle ending |Passive participle ending|
|--|--|--|
|past |-int |-it|
|present |-ant |-at|
|future |-ont |-ot|
注意元音iao现在、过去、将来时中是等价的。（应该意思是说用iao元音来区分三个时态，这三个元音同样起区分时态的作用）

##主动分词
现在主动分词相当于英语中的-ing。跟英语不同的是，现在主动分词在世界语中也有过去、将来时态：
|Tense |Participle |Translation|
|--|--|--|
|present |dormanta |sleeping|
|past |dorminta |having slept|
|future |dormonta |about to sleep|

例子：
|Tense |Esperanto |"literal explanation" |English|
|--|--|--|--|
|present |La kato estas dormanta ||The cat is sleeping.|
|past |La kato estas dorminta |"The cat is having slept" |The cat has slept.|
|future |La kato estas dormonta |"The cat (at this present moment)is about to sleep" |The cat is about to sleep.|

##被动分词
先看两个英语句子：
- The owl ate the mouse.
- The mouse was eaten by the owl.
虽然表达的是同一个意思，但第二句中老鼠是叙述重点，是主语。第一句用的是主动动词ate，第二句则是被动动词was eaten
被动分词的例子：
|Tense |Esperanto |"literal translation" |English|
|--|--|--|--|
|present |La libro estas legata. ||The book is being read.|
|past |La libro estas legita |"The book is having been read" |The book has been read.|
|future |La libro estas legota |"The book is about to be read" |The book is about to be read.|
##分词作形容词
以a结尾的分词跟形容词一样的用法：
- Mi estas skribanta. = I am (in the process of) writing.
- Ni estas skribantaj. = We are (in the process of) writing.
- La libro estas legita. = The book (literally: is having been read) has been read.
- La libroj estas legitaj. = The books (literally: are having been read) have been read.
- Mi vidis kurantan viron. = I saw a man (in the process of) running / I saw a running man.
- Mi trovis du rompitajn po?telefonojn. = I found two mobile phones (literally: having been broken) that had been broken / I found two broken mobile phones.

##更多关于分词
分词也可以和各种时态的esti组合使用：
Examples with an active participle（主动分词）:
Li estas leganta la libron
He is (in the process of) reading the book.
Li estos leginta la libron.
(literally: He will be having read the book)
He will have read the book.
Li estas leginta la libron.
(literally: He is having read the book.)
He has read (has finished reading) the book.
Li estis leginta la libron.
(literally: He was having read the book)
He had read the book.

Examples with a passive participle（被动分词）:
La libro estos legita.
(literally: The book will be having been read.)
The book will have been read.
La libro estas legita.
(literally: The book is having been read.)
The book has been read.
La libro estis legita.
(literally: The book was having been read.)
The book had been read.
注意：以上这些对应英语翻译为了便于理解，有意思地加了分词用法。但实际上其并非标准通俗翻译，英语一般并不会那么说话

##以-o结尾分词
-o结尾分词通常指人：
- la leganto - the reader
- la skribanto - the writer
- la gvidonto - the future guide
- la elektito - the one who got elected
- la konato - the one who is known, the acquaintance
- La can take the place of possessive pronouns

#ideas
##后缀 -eco
英语中有各种后缀创建抽象名词，世界语中统一使用-eco：
- boneco - goodness
- patrineco - motherhood
- libereco - freedom
- amikeco - friendship
- egaleco - equality
- ri?eco - wealth

##La放在一些抽象名词前。
定冠词la通常被用在抽象名词前：
- la amo - not one specific person's love for another, but love in the abstract sense
- la kulturo - not one specific culture, but culture in the general or abstract sense
- la espero - not my hope to receive a gift tomorrow, but the idea of hope in the abstract
